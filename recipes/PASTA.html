Pasta recipe

Description

Italian pasta (pronounced “PAS-tah”) is a collective name for a category of food made from wheat flour and water, sometimes with egg. The name refers to the resulting dough (pasta also means “dough”) that is rolled out and cut into various shapes. Pasta is cooked in water and served with a sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Whipped Cream
  • Chicken
  • Soy sauce
  • Chilli sauce

Steps To Make Pasta

  1. Boil Water. Start with a very large pot of water, about 6 quarts per pound of pasta.
  2. Add Salt. Put in a lot of salt, about 3 tablespoons.
  3. Add the Pasta.
  4. Stir.
  5. Taste the Pasta.
  6. Drain.
  7. Removing Ravioli.
  8. Stir In the Sauce
  9. Add Pasta Water
  10. Pasta is served!!
